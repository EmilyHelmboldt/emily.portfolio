Why Hire Me?

With over a decade of experience as an educator, curriculum designer, and writer, I have dedicated my career to creating meaningful learning experiences and engaging content. My passion lies in developing innovative and accessible educational materials that empower students and support educators.

My Background

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in English with a minor in History from Western Michigan University. My diverse experience includes:

  • High School English Teacher: Over 10 years of classroom experience teaching literature, writing, and communication skills.
  • Curriculum Developer: Designed standards-based curricula for English and World History, creating engaging lessons tailored to diverse learning needs.
  • Passport Specialist at the U.S. Department of State: Developed expertise in government policies, documentation, and critical analysis.
  • Academic & Creative Writer: Skilled in research-based content writing, instructional design, and creative storytelling.
  • Varsity Tutors Educator: Provided one-on-one academic support in writing, history, and standardized test preparation.

  • Adaptability & Collaboration: Adept at working in diverse educational settings and collaborating with teams to achieve learning objectives.
  • Commitment to Excellence: Dedicated to delivering high-quality, engaging, and accessible learning materials.
